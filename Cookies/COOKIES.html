This Cookie Policy explains how our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. By continuing to use our website, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device by websites you visit. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to the owners of the site.

Types of Cookies We Use

Our website uses the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic functions like page navigation and access to secure areas of the website. The website cannot function properly without these cookies.
  • Analytics Cookies: These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously.
  • Marketing Cookies: These cookies are used to track visitors across websites. The intention is to display ads that are relevant and engaging for the individual user.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can manage your cookie preferences by adjusting the settings in your browser. Please note that blocking certain types of cookies may impact your experience on our website.
